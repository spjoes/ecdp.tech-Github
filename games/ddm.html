Mac de DS (マックでDS)


Mac de DS (マックでDS) was a service in McDonald's across Japan. Ds De Mac allowed customers to go to several McDonald's across Japan to download exclusive games or take part in limited time events. These events include things like Pokemon distributions as well as things like Ds Mac Adventures.
